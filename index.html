Meitar Ohayon

My name is Meitar Ohayon.
I am a pastry chef, chocolatier, and instructor of baking workshops.
Over the years I have gained a lot of knowledge in a variety of fields:
showcase desserts, restaurant desserts, baking bread, working with Tabon,
ice cream production, service management, recruiting.
In addition, I own an independent business of designed birthday cakes.

In the last seven years, I have been involved in the patisserie business.
I wanted to learn another field in order to reach a place where I have options for advancement and growth,
to enter a new field, and expand my responsibilities.
I found a great interest in the QA (Quality Assurance) world and points of interface between the two fields.
In patisserie, attention to detail and perfectionism are required to succeed,
focusing on the small "bugs" in a cake or dessert until the product is perfect before it goes to the customer.
The same goes for QA, the requirement is quite similar, so I became curious and drawn into this world.
